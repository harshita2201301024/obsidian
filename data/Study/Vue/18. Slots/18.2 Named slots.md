Later, you realize that the title can also be some complex HTML. Of course, there is a way to pass multiple contents to the card component, using multiple named slots.

```js
<template>
<div class="card">
<div class="card-body">
<h4 class="card-title">
<slot name="title"></slot>
</h4>
<p class="card-text">
<slot></slot>
</p>
</div>
</div>
</template>
```
